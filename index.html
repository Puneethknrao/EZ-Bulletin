    • WELCOME

    To Voice Controlled News App

    We understand you don’t have time to go through multiple news articles everyday from different sources. So we cut the clutter and deliver them, in a single page.

    This is a simple and easy way to keep track of the headlines generated by different news sources on various trending topics.

    Voice assistance integrated in this app provides a very good user experience.

    INSTRUCTIONS

    • To read the news article, try saying "Read the news"
    • To pause reading the news, say "Pause"
    • To resume reading the news, say "Resume"
    • To stop reading the news, say "Stop"
    • To display news on particular topics, say "Technology/Entertainment/Business/general news"
    • To open a particular article, say "open article 1/2/3...."
